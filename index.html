Q2: Pricing, Promotion & Revenue Optimization

This quarter tackles the highest-leverage question in business: how much should we charge? You'll move from measuring price sensitivity through conjoint-based willingness-to-pay to dynamic pricing algorithms, promotion ROI, and the causal methods that separate real lift from noise.

📈

Price Elasticity

Log-log regression, arc elasticity, cross-price effects — measure how demand responds to every dollar of price change.

⚖

Conjoint & WTP

Choice-based conjoint analysis, part-worth utilities, willingness-to-pay — decompose what customers really value in dollars.

⚡

Dynamic Pricing

Revenue management, EMSR, Thompson Sampling bandits — optimize prices in real-time as demand signals evolve.

🎯

Promotion Science

Uplift modeling, PSM, intervention analysis — measure true incremental lift and stop bleeding profit on discounts.
